About GroqSeeker_Bot

GroqSeeker_Bot is a powerful AI assistant that gives you access to multiple AI models and DBS price prediction directly in Telegram.

Available Commands

  • /start - Welcome message and basic instructions
  • /help - List of available commands
  • /llama <question> - Chat with LLAMA 3.1 8B Instant model
  • /deepseek <question> - Chat with Deepseek R1 Distill LLAMA 70B model
  • /predict <usdsgd> - Predict DBS share price based on USD/SGD rate
  • /reset - Reset your conversation history

Features

  • Multi-turn conversations - The bot remembers your chat history
  • Multiple AI models - Choose between LLAMA and Deepseek for different tasks
  • DBS price prediction - Get predictions based on USD/SGD exchange rate
  • Smart memory management - Automatically handles long conversations
