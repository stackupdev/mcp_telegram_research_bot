About Deep Research Bot

Deep Research Bot is a powerful AI assistant that helps you conduct comprehensive research by generating structured research prompts for AI assistants like Claude and ChatGPT.

Available Commands

  • /start - Welcome message and basic instructions
  • /help - List of available commands
  • /llama <question> - Chat with LLAMA 3.1 8B Instant model
  • /deepseek <question> - Chat with Deepseek R1 Distill LLAMA 70B model
  • /research <question> - Generate a comprehensive research prompt
  • /get_prompt - Get the full research prompt for external AI use
  • /research_status - Check current research status and summary
  • /clear_research - Clear current research data
  • /reset - Reset your conversation history

Features

  • Multi-turn conversations - The bot remembers your chat history
  • Multiple AI models - Choose between LLAMA and Deepseek for different tasks
  • Deep research prompts - Generate structured research workflows for AI assistants
  • Research state management - Track and manage your research questions and progress
  • Smart memory management - Automatically handles long conversations
