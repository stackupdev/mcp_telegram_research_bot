About InquisitaSparkBot

InquisitaSparkBot is an advanced AI assistant for academic research and LLM chat, available directly in Telegram. Search and organize papers, chat with state-of-the-art models, and manage research topicsâ€”all powered by a remote MCP server.

Available Commands

  • /start - Welcome message and basic instructions
  • /help - List of available commands
  • /llama <question> - Chat with LLAMA 3.1 8B Instant model
  • /deepseek <question> - Chat with Deepseek R1 Distill LLAMA 70B model
  • /search <topic> - Search academic papers on a topic
  • /papers <topic> - View papers for a topic
  • /topics - List available research topics
  • /reset - Reset your conversation history

Features

  • Academic paper research - Search and organize papers by topic (powered by remote MCP server)
  • Multi-turn conversations - The bot remembers your chat history
  • Multiple AI models - Choose between LLAMA and Deepseek for different tasks
  • Smart memory management - Automatically handles long conversations
